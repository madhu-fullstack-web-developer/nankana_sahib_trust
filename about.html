Nankana Sahib Education Trust (NSET) was founded to provide quality education and technological advancements. With a strong commitment to academic excellence, GNDEC was established in 1956, becoming a pioneer in technical education.

Our Journey

1953

Polytechnic was started to provide technical education.

1956

Guru Nanak Dev Engineering College (GNDEC) was established.

2004

Courses accredited by NBA, New Delhi.

2012

GNDEC granted Autonomous Status under UGC Act.

Present

ISO 9001-2008 certified, accredited with 'A' grade by NAAC.
