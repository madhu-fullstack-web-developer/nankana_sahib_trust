About Community Development & Training Centre

Established in 1999, the Community Development & Training Centre is dedicated to empowering marginalized communities by providing vocational training in stitching, tailoring, and computer applications. With a strong focus on skill development, the center enhances employability and promotes economic independence, particularly among women and youth. Through hands-on training and practical education, it equips individuals with the necessary skills to secure sustainable livelihoods. By fostering self-reliance and entrepreneurship, the center plays a pivotal role in uplifting rural communities. Its commitment to education and skill-building ensures long-term social and economic growth, making a meaningful impact on countless lives across the region.

Training & Development Facilities

Established in 1999, the Community Development & Training Centre provides a dynamic learning environment focused on vocational training and skill development. The center is equipped with dedicated training labs for stitching, tailoring, and computer applications, ensuring hands-on experience for trainees. With a strong emphasis on women's empowerment, the institution offers specialized programs to enhance economic independence and self-sufficiency. Community outreach initiatives, workshops, and career guidance sessions help bridge the gap between education and employability. The center remains committed to uplifting marginalized communities by fostering skills, confidence, and sustainable livelihoods for individuals striving for a better future.
