About GNDPC

Founded in 1953, Guru Nanak Dev Polytechnic College is the first institution under NSET, dedicated to providing quality technical education. It offers diploma courses in Civil, Electrical, and Mechanical Engineering, focusing on skill development and practical learning. With state-of-the-art workshops and laboratories, the college equips students with hands-on training and industrial exposure, preparing them for real-world challenges. Emphasizing innovation and technical expertise, it plays a crucial role in empowering rural youth, bridging the gap between education and industry needs. The institution fosters entrepreneurial skills, ensuring students graduate with the confidence and knowledge to excel in their careers.

Campus Facilities

Guru Nanak Dev Polytechnic College offers a dynamic learning environment with modern workshops, advanced laboratories, and smart classrooms, ensuring practical and skill-based education. The institution emphasizes technical excellence through hands-on training and industry-oriented programs that bridge the gap between education and real-world applications. Beyond academics, it promotes holistic development with a dedicated sports complex, fostering teamwork and physical well-being. Regular workshops, industrial visits, and seminars enhance students' creativity, leadership, and technical expertise. Committed to innovation and excellence, the college empowers students with the skills and knowledge needed to excel in their careers and contribute meaningfully to the industry.
