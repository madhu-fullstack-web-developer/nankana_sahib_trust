About

Established in 1953, Nankana Sahib Education Trust (NSET) was founded with the noble mission of uplifting rural communities through quality education. Guided by the vision of "Removal of Economic Backwardness through Technology" the trust has been instrumental in providing education to thousands of students, especially from rural backgrounds. With 18 Educational Institutions under its umbrella, including Nanak Dev Engineering College, Guru Nanak Dev Polytechnic, and Nankana Sahib Public School, NSET continues to empower young minds by offering a blend of academic excellence, innovation, and social values.

Our Institutions

Guru Nanak Dev Engineering College(GNDEC)

Established in 1956, GNDEC is one of the oldest and prestigious engineering colleges in Northern India. The institution is ISO Certified, NBA Accredited, and conferred Autonomous Status by UGC. With a legacy of producing 10,000+ Engineers, GNDEC plays a key role in Technical Education and Research. It has received grants from TEQIP-II, DST, and AICTE for various research projects. The college collaborates with IOWA University USA and promotes Innovation and Entrepreneurship among students.

Guru Nanak Dev Polytechnic

Founded in 1953, this Polytechnic Institute is the First Institution under NSET. It offers Diploma Courses in Engineering and focuses on Skill Development and Practical Learning for rural youth. The institute provides courses in Civil, Electrical, and Mechanical Engineering. It has well-equipped workshops and laboratories to promote hands-on training. The Polytechnic plays a vital role in empowering rural students with technical skills. The institute conducts Industrial Training Programs to bridge the gap between education and industry needs.

Nankana Sahib Public School

Established in 1978, Nankana Sahib Public School provides Quality Education on the Public School Pattern. Spread across 15 acres of land, the school caters to 60 villages around Ludhiana. It follows the CBSE Curriculum and promotes Holistic Development of students. The school organizes Cultural, Sports, and Community Programs to enhance student participation. With a team of highly qualified teachers, the school empowers students to excel in academics and co-curricular activities.

Training College Kot Gangu Rai

Training College Kot Gangu Rai provides Teacher Education Programs to produce highly skilled educators. The college offers Diploma and Degree Courses in Education to uplift the rural society. It promotes Women Empowerment by enrolling female candidates in large numbers. The college organizes Workshops, Seminars, and Community Outreach Programs. It plays a key role in strengthening the educational infrastructure in rural areas.

Community Development & Training Centre

Started in 1999, the Community Development & Training Centre aims to uplift the weaker sections of society. It offers Vocational Training Programs in areas like Stitching, Tailoring, and Computer Applications. The centre empowers Women and Youth by providing Skill Development Courses. It serves as a platform to enhance Employability and Economic Independence among rural communities.

Science & Technology Entrepreneur’s Park (STEP)

STEP was established to foster Entrepreneurship, Innovation, and Research Projects. It provides Technical Infrastructure and Business Incubation to budding entrepreneurs. The park encourages Startups in Technology and Agriculture sectors. STEP plays a pivotal role in promoting Innovation and Self-Employment among students and the rural population.
