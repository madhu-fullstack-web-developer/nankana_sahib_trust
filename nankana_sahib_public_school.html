About Nankana Sahib Public School

Established in 1978, Nankana Sahib Public School is committed to providing quality education under the CBSE curriculum. Spread across 15 acres, the school serves students from 60 villages around Ludhiana, ensuring inclusive and accessible learning. With a focus on holistic development, it offers a well-balanced approach to academics, sports, and cultural activities. The school fosters intellectual growth, creativity, and leadership through expert faculty and modern teaching methodologies. Various community programs, extracurricular activities, and sports initiatives help students develop discipline, teamwork, and confidence. Dedicated to academic excellence and moral values, the school prepares students for a bright future.

School Infrastructure

Nankana Sahib Public School provides a vibrant learning environment with modern classrooms, well-equipped science and computer labs, and a vast library for intellectual growth. The school promotes holistic development through cultural, sports, and co-curricular activities. With a dedicated sports complex, playgrounds, and activity centers, students enhance their physical and creative skills. The institution regularly organizes competitions, educational trips, and interactive workshops to encourage learning beyond textbooks. Committed to academic excellence and character building, the school ensures that every student is well-prepared to succeed in both academics and life.
