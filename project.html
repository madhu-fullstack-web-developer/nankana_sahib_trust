Projects of Nankana Sahib Education Trust (NSET)

Introduction

Nankana Sahib Education Trust (NSET) has been at the forefront of academic excellence, research, and community development. Over the years, the trust has undertaken several projects aimed at fostering technological advancements, skill development, and social upliftment. These projects cater to students, researchers, and the wider community, contributing to the mission of "Removal of Economic Backwardness through Technology.

Our key Projects

Technical Education Quality Improvement Programme-II (TEQIP-II)

TEQIP-II was sanctioned by the Ministry of Human Resource Development (MHRD), Government of India, with funding of ₹10 Crores. It aimed at improving learning outcomes, faculty development, and research facilities.
