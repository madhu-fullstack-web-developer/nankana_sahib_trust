About STEP

The Science & Technology Entrepreneur’s Park (STEP) was established to drive entrepreneurship, innovation, and research. It provides essential technical infrastructure and business incubation support to budding entrepreneurs, fostering the growth of startups in technology and agriculture. By bridging the gap between education and industry, STEP empowers students and the rural population with the skills and resources needed for self-employment. The park plays a vital role in promoting a culture of innovation, offering mentorship, networking opportunities, and hands-on training. Through its initiatives, STEP nurtures creative problem-solving, economic development, and sustainable entrepreneurship, contributing to a thriving business ecosystem.

Infrastructure & Facilities

Science & Technology Entrepreneur’s Park (STEP) offers a thriving ecosystem for entrepreneurship and innovation. The facility includes advanced research labs, co-working spaces, and a dedicated business incubation center to support emerging startups. STEP provides access to cutting-edge technology, mentorship programs, and industry collaborations, fostering a culture of creativity and self-employment. Entrepreneurs benefit from networking opportunities, funding assistance, and hands-on training in business development. With a focus on technology and agricultural advancements, STEP plays a crucial role in empowering students and rural entrepreneurs, equipping them with the resources to turn their ideas into successful ventures.
